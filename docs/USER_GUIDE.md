# User Guide

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the registration page
2. Fill in your information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Click "Create Account"
4. Verify your email address (check your inbox)

### Logging In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

You can also use social login options:
- Google
- Facebook
- Apple

## Finding Communities

### Browse Communities

1. Click "Communities" in the navigation
2. Browse the list or use the map view
3. Use filters to narrow your search:
   - Category (Running, Yoga, Cycling, etc.)
   - Location
   - Price range

### Search Communities

1. Use the search bar to find communities by name
2. Use advanced search for more options:
   - Location with radius
   - Category filters
   - Price range
   - Sort by popularity, distance, or price

### Join a Community

1. Click on a community to view details
2. Click "Join Community"
3. If the community requires a membership, you'll be redirected to purchase

## Creating a Community

### Step 1: Basic Information

1. Click "Create Community" from your dashboard
2. Enter community name
3. Select a category

### Step 2: Description

1. Add a description of your community
2. This helps members understand what your community is about

### Step 3: Location

1. Enter your community's address
2. The system will automatically geocode the location

### Step 4: Privacy Settings

Choose your privacy level:
- **Public:** Anyone can find and join
- **Private:** Only members can see
- **Invite Only:** Requires an invitation

### Step 5: Review and Create

1. Review all information
2. Click "Create Community"

## Managing Events

### Creating an Event

1. Navigate to your community
2. Click "Create Event"
3. Fill in event details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Date and time
   - Location
   - Capacity (optional)
   - Pricing (free or paid)
4. Click "Create Event"

### RSVP to Events

1. Browse events on the Events page
2. Click on an event to view details
3. Click "RSVP" to confirm attendance
4. For paid events, complete the payment process

### Managing RSVPs

As an organizer:
1. Go to your event
2. View the attendee list
3. Manage waitlist if event is full
4. Check in attendees at the event

## Memberships

### Purchasing a Membership

1. Navigate to a community
2. Click "Join Community"
3. Select a membership tier:
   - Basic
   - Premium
   - VIP
4. Complete payment
5. Start enjoying membership benefits

### Managing Your Memberships

1. Go to "My Memberships" from your profile
2. View all your active memberships
3. Cancel or upgrade as needed

## Leaderboards

### Viewing Leaderboards

1. Click "Leaderboards" in navigation
2. Choose between:
   - **Local:** Rankings within a specific community
   - **Global:** Rankings across all communities
3. Filter by time period:
   - Daily
   - Weekly
   - Monthly
   - All-time

### Earning Points

Points are earned by:
- Attending events
- Checking in at events
- Participating in community activities
- Completing challenges

## Profile Settings

### Updating Your Profile

1. Go to "Profile" from the navigation
2. Update your information:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Avatar
3. Click "Save Changes"

### Account Settings

1. Go to "Settings"
2. Change password
3. Update privacy preferences
4. Manage email notifications

## Analytics (Organizers)

### Viewing Analytics

1. Navigate to your community
2. Click "Analytics"
3. View metrics:
   - Member count
   - Event statistics
   - Revenue
   - Growth trends

### Exporting Data

1. Go to Analytics dashboard
2. Click "Export Data"
3. Choose format (CSV or PDF)
4. Download your report
